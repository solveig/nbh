<h1>X3DOM Text Example</h1>
<p>The following scenes demonstrate the use of the <tt>text</tt> and <tt>fontsyle</tt> nodes. You can also use web fonts, however on windows there is a glitch. You have to use and <span class="webfont">display the font in your document</span> before you can use them in X3DOM.</p>

</appearance>
<text string='Times 32px' solid='false'>
<fontstyle family="'Times' 'Orbitron'" size="0.8"></fontstyle>
